# Sea Dogs Privacy Policy

**Effective date: July 6, 2026**

Sea Dogs ("the app") is a marine conditions app for iOS. This policy explains what information the app uses and what happens to it. The short version: Sea Dogs has no accounts, no ads, no analytics, and no tracking. Almost everything stays on your device.

## Information the app uses

**Your location (optional).** The app only reads your device location when you tap "Use my current location." Your coordinates are used to fetch a marine forecast and to look up a nearby place name. Your location is never read in the background, never used for advertising, and never linked to your identity. If you prefer, you can skip location entirely and search for a harbor by name instead.

**Forecast requests.** To show conditions, the app sends the coordinates of your selected location (or a place name you type into search) to Open-Meteo, a weather data provider. These requests contain no account information, device identifiers, or personal details — only the coordinates or search text needed to return a forecast. Open-Meteo's privacy policy is available at [open-meteo.com](https://open-meteo.com/en/terms).

**Photos (optional).** If you create a Sea Report, the app uses a photo you take or choose from your library. Photos are stored only on your device. They are never uploaded to us or anyone else. A photo leaves your device only if you choose to share it using the iOS share sheet, and then only to the app or person you select.

**Purchases.** Premium subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through your Apple ID. We never see or store your name, payment details, or billing information. You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime in your device Settings.

## Information stored on your device

The app saves your selected location, unit preference, chosen app icon, onboarding status, saved Sea Reports, and a copy of the latest forecast for the home-screen widgets. All of this lives only on your device (and in your device backups, if enabled). Deleting the app deletes all of it.

## What we do NOT do

- No user accounts or sign-in
- No analytics or usage tracking
- No advertising or ad networks
- No selling, renting, or sharing of any data
- No data collection linked to your identity

## Children

Sea Dogs is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

## Safety disclaimer

Forecasts, modeled tides, and modeled currents shown in the app are numerical model guidance only and must not be used for navigation. Always consult official sources (such as NOAA products and local notices to mariners) before heading out.
